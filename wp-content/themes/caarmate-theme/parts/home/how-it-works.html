How CaarMate Works

🔍

1. Search or Post

Passengers enter their destination to find a match. Drivers publish their route and available seats.

✅

2. Book & Confirm

Review the trip details, securely book your seat, and instantly connect with your travel mate.

🚗

3. Share the Load

Meet up, enjoy the ride, and split the travel costs. It's smart, eco-friendly, and cost-effective.
